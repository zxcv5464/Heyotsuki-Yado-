# Image Mapping

Original files are preserved outside the deployment assets under `_image_backup/originals/`.

Conversion settings:

- Staff photos: WebP quality 82.
- Menu photos: WebP quality 82, or lossless when transparency is present.
- Backgrounds: WebP quality 75.
- Logos and images with actual transparent pixels: lossless WebP.
- All GIF sources were static single-frame files, so no animation was lost.
